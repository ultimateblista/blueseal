FLAGSHIP FACILITY

Blue Seal Chemical Production Plant Ltd

$12.5M Manufacturing Facility

Our world-class production facility in Ibeju-Lekki, Lagos — the beating heart of Blue Seal's manufacturing operations. Producing up to 5 million litres per day across oilfield, water treatment, environmental, and specialty chemicals.

The plant represents the largest indigenous chemical manufacturing investment in West Africa, featuring state-of-the-art equipment and processes that meet international quality standards.
