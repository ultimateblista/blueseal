OUR JOURNEY

From Trading to Manufacturing

Founded in 2006 as a trading company, Blue Seal Energy Group has grown into Africa's leading indigenous chemical manufacturer — building the $12.5 million Blue Seal Chemical Production Plant in Ibeju-Lekki, Lagos, with a clear continental mandate.

Our first employees, Josephine and Tonya, embodied the founding spirit: local expertise, global standards. Today, we employ over 500 people directly and support more than 2,000 direct and indirect jobs.

We operate as part of an 8-company North American group, bringing international networks and institutional rigour to African industrial development.

OUR PURPOSE

The Mission

Blue Seal exists to re-industrialise Africa from within — building world-class manufacturing capacity, creating high-value jobs, and proving that African companies can compete globally without compromise.
