Who We Are

A Pan-African
Industrial Powerhouse

Blue Seal Energy Group is Africa's leading indigenous chemical manufacturer, operating a world-class $12.5 million production facility in Ibeju-Lekki, Lagos — and expanding rapidly across the continent.

Founded in 2006, we have grown from a trading company into a full-fledged chemical manufacturer, serving over 20 industries across oil & gas, water management, agriculture, and environmental services.
